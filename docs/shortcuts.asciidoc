=== Keyboard Shortcuts

Here is a complete list of keyboard shortcuts:

 - *Bold* (type `ctrl+b`)
 - _Italics_ (type `ctrl+i`)
 - `Code` (type `ctrl+shift+c`)
 - `Single Quote' (type `ctrl+'`)
 - ``Double Quotes'' (type `ctrl+shift+'`)
 - ^Superscript^ (type `ctrl+shift+6`)
 - ~Subscript~ (type `ctrl+shift+\``)
 - `Command` (type `ctrl+\``)
